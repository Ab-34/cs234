REWARD HACKING:

- The agent learns that simply tilting the cube raises the height of its center and treats that as a success
- Corrected by introducing a dense and continuous reward for lifting higher
- In another video, the agent learnt to simply align itself with the cube at a height above it and close its grippers fully to maximise grasping reward

OCCLUSIONS:

- VLM based rewards do not work when the block is occluded
- Also leads to alignment issues

GRASPS BUT DOES NOT LIFT:

- The reward continuity after grasping needs to guide the agent towards exploring lifting
- Densifying the lifting process and pre training with BC solves this
